Project Overview
Everything You Need to Know
Read this before you start. It covers what the project is, how it's graded, and what's due each day.
The Mission
Working with a partner, you'll select a 7th grade math standard and design a complete mini-lesson around it. You'll break down the standard, plan how to teach it, build slides with speaker notes, design a student activity, and create a way to check for understanding. On presentation day you'll teach your lesson live to the class.
